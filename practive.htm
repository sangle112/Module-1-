Tulsa Car Accident Attorney

If you or a loved one have been in an auto accident, you may be feeling overwhelmed and frustrated. The aftermath of a car accident can affect your personal and professional life as you deal with physical, emotional, and financial burdens that an accident can cause.

Causes of Automobiles Accidents

Sometimes the cause of an accident is a driver's actions or in-actions and may not be obvious. Common causes include:

  • Distracted driving
  • Driving aggressively
  • Drunk driving
  • Fatigued driving
  • Speaking or texting with a cell phone

How We Will handle Your Case?

When we meet with new clients, our priority is to establish a strong attorney-client relationship. This will help us collaborate with you throughout the entire legal process, and make you feel comfortable coming to us with any questions or concerns along the way.

Compensation

If you have suffered harm and losses in a car accident, you have the right to make a claim for compensation for:

  • Medical expenses
  • Out-of-pocket expenses
  • Property damage
  • Loss of wages
  • Physical pain and suffering
